Why Quality Matters

  • Unique bioactive properties not found in other honeys.
  • Quality variations impact potency and effectiveness.
  • High-quality Manuka honey ensures you experience its full potential.

Understanding the Key Markers

UMF™ Certification

The most trusted grading system measuring Methylglyoxal (MGO) content, a key bioactive compound.

MGO Rating

Indicates potency, with higher levels offering stronger potential benefits.

Pollen Analysis

Reveals the percentage of Manuka pollen, ensuring authenticity and source.

Diastase Activity

Measures natural enzymes, reflecting freshness and proper handling.

Hydroxymethylfurfural (HMF) Content

Lower levels signify higher quality, indicating proper storage and handling.
